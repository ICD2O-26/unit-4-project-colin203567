IoT (Internet of Things) Smart Home

What Is IoT Smart Home Technology?

IoT connects everyday devices to the internet.

Some examples of Internet Connected Devices are:

Toasters, Fridges, Lights, Locks, Thermostats, Alarm systems, Doorbells

What Problem Does It Solve?

IoT improves:

  • Convenience – control devices in your home from your phone
  • Security – smart locks, cameras, alerts
  • Energy efficiency – smart thermostats and lights
  • Accessibility – voice control for people with disabilities

Examples of Real IoT Smart Home Devices

  • Google Nest Learning Thermostat
  • Amazon Echo (Alexa)
  • Ring Video Doorbell
  • Philips Hue Smart Lights
  • August Wi‑Fi Smart Lock

How Does It Work?

IoT devices use sensors and wireless networks to communicate.
