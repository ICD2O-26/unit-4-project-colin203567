What Is IoT Smart Home Technology?

IoT smart home technology connects everyday devices—lights, locks, thermostats, speakers— to the internet so they can automate tasks and communicate with each other.

What Problem Does It Solve?

IoT improves:

  • Convenience
  • Security
  • Energy efficiency
  • Accessibility

How Does It Work?

IoT devices use sensors and wireless networks to collect data and send it to apps or cloud systems.

Some smart homes use AI to predict your routines—lights, temperature, and music adjust automatically.

Social & Global Impacts

Social: More convenience and accessibility.

Cultural: Homes feel more futuristic and automated.

Economic: Creates new tech jobs.

Environmental: Reduces energy waste.

Ethical: Raises privacy concerns.

Global: Expands automation worldwide.
